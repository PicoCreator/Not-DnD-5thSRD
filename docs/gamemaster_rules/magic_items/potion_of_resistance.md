# Elixir of Resistance
_Elixir, uncommon_

When you imbibe this elixir, you gain resistance to one type of harm for 1 hour. The GM shall choose the type or randomly determine it from the options below.

| d10 | Damage Type |
|-----|-------------|
| 1   | Acid        |
| 2   | Cold        |
| 3   | Fire        |
| 4   | Force       |
| 5   | Lightning   |
| 6   | Necrotic    |
| 7   | Poison      |
| 8   | Psychic     |
| 9   | Radiant     |
| 10  | Thunder     |
