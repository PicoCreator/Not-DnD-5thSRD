# Band of Resistance
_Ring, rare (requires attunement)_

When wearing this ring, you have resistance to one type of damage. The gem in the ring will indicate the type, which the GM will choose or determine randomly.

| d10 | Damage Type | Gem        |
|-----|-------------|------------|
| 1   | Acid        | Pearl      |
| 2   | Cold        | Tourmaline |
| 3   | Fire        | Garnet     |
| 4   | Force       | Sapphire   |
| 5   | Lightning   | Citrine    |
| 6   | Necrotic    | Jet        |
| 7   | Poison      | Amethyst   |
| 8   | Psychic     | Jade       |
| 9   | Radiant     | Topaz      |
| 10  | Thunder     | Spinel     |
