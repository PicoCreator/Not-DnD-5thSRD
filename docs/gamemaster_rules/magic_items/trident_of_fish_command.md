# Trident of Aquatic Dominion
_Weapon (trident), uncommon (requires attunement)_

This trident is a magical weapon, imbued with powerful magicks. It holds three charges, and while it is in your possession, you may expend one charge to cast **_Dominate Beast_** (save DC 15) upon any beast with an innate swimming speed. The Trident of Aquatic Dominion will regain one to three expended charges daily at the break of dawn.
