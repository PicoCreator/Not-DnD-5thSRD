# Berserker 
_Medium humanoid (any race), any chaotic alignment_

**Armour Class** 13 (hide armour)    
**Hit Points** 67 (9d8 + 27)    
**Speed** 30 ft. 

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 17 (+3) | 9 (−1)  | 11 (+0) | 9 (−1)  |  

**Senses** passive Perception 10    
**Languages** any one language (usually Common)    
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP) 

**Frenzied.** At the start of its turn, the berserker can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn. 

### Actions 
**Greataxe.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage. 

### Description
From the wilds of the untamed lands, the berserker comes forth in a rage, seeking out conflict and battle wherever they can find it. With reckless abandon, they swing their greataxe with a ferocity that few can match.
