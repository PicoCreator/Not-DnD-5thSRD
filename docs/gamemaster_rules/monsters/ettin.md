# Ogre
_Large giant, chaotic evil_

**Armour Class** 12 (natural armour)    
**Hit Points** 85 (10d10 + 30)    
**Speed** 40 ft. 

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 21 (+5) | 8 (−1) | 17 (+3) | 6 (−2) | 10 (+0) | 8 (−1) |

**Skills** Perception +4    
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14    
**Languages** Giant, Orc    
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP) 

**Two Heads.** The ogre has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks and on saving throws against being blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.    
**Wakeful.** When one of the ogre's heads is asleep, its other head is awake. 

### Actions 
**Multiattack.** The ogre makes two attacks: one with its battleaxe and one with its morningstar.    
**Battleaxe.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 14 (2d8 + 5) slashing damage.    
**Morningstar.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 14 (2d8 + 5) piercing damage.
