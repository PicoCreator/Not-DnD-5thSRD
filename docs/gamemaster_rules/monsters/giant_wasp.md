Behold the Giant Wasp, a medium-sized beast of unaligned nature. It is protected by an armor of 12, and has 13 hit points (3d8). It moves at a speed of 10 feet, and can fly at a speed of 50 feet. Its strength, dexterity, constitution, intelligence, wisdom, and charisma are all 10 (+0), 14 (+2), 10 (+0), 1 (−5), 10 (+0), and 3 (−4) respectively. It has a passive Perception of 10, and is a challenge of 1/2 (100 XP).

The Giant Wasp has one action: its sting. It can make a melee weapon attack with a +4 to hit, reaching 5 feet, and dealing 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage. The target must make a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is stable but poisoned for 1 hour, even after regaining hit points, and is paralyzed while poisoned in this way.
