# Leaping 
_1st-level transmutation_ 

**Casting Time:** 1 action    
**Range:** Touch    
**Components:** V, S, M (a grasshopper's hind leg)    
**Duration:** 1 minute 

You lay your hand upon a creature. Until the spell ends, the creature's leaping distance is tripled.
