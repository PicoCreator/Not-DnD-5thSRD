# Mind Blank 
_8th-level abjuration_ 

**Casting Time:** One action    
**Range:** Touch    
**Components:** V, S    
**Duration:** Twenty-four hours 

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is safeguarded from psychic damage, any effect that would sense its emotions or peruse its thoughts, divination spells, and the charmed condition. The spell even **_thwarts_** wish spells and spells or effects of similar might used to influence the target's mind or to gain knowledge of the target.
