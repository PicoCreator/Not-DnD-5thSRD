# Ethereal Step 
_2nd-level conjuration_ 

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action    
**Range:** Self    
**Components:** V    
**Duration:** Instantaneous 

You are briefly surrounded by a shimmering mist, allowing you to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see.
